Synopsis

Our platform provides a dedicated tool for crafting a concise and compelling synopsis. This feature allows students to summarize their project’s objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes in a clear, structured format. It includes pre-designed templates for a professional and organized synopsis, guided sections for project overview and research questions, and tips for writing a focused summary. The synopsis feature also integrates seamlessly with the full research report to ensure consistency.
